About KG

Your trusted partner for procurement excellence across South Africa

Khavho Group (KG) is the e-commerce procurement division of the Khavho Group, a dynamic and forward-thinking conglomerate operating across multiple sectors in South Africa.

We specialize in providing comprehensive procurement solutions for construction, electrical, mechanical, and general business supplies. Our platform enables both B2B and B2C customers to efficiently source quality materials and equipment with the reliability and service excellence that defines the Khavho Group.

Operating primarily in Limpopo and Gauteng, we leverage our extensive network and centralized logistics to deliver exceptional value to our customers across South Africa.
